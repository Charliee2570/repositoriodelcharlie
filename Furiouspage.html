Fast and Furious

SuperFast

Genre: Comedy

Duration: 139 minutes

Director: Jason Friedberg

Synopsis: Undercover cop Lucas White joins the illegal street racing gang of Vin Serento in Los Angeles. They are fast and furious and plan to betray Los Angeles crime boss Juan Carlos de la Sol, who hides his cash in a Taco Bell downtown. The gang's outrageous plan is as bold as it is ridiculous, and it will have them dragging the whole damn restaurant at crazy speeds.
