Genre: Comedy

Synopsis: At twenty years old, Ashtray is no longer a child. Because of this, his mother considers him an adult and decides to send him back to live with his father. Once he moves to South Central to start a new life with his father and grandmother, he faces several dilemmas: his cousin Loc Dog wants to recruit him for his criminal gang, and an attractive single mother named Dashiki comes into his life.
